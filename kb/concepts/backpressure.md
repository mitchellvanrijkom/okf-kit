# Backpressure
Backpressure prevents unbounded queues by signalling upstream producers to slow down when a consumer falls behind. It is closely related to [rate limiting](/concepts/rate-limiting.md). A missing link on purpose: [see the missing page](/concepts/nonexistent.md).
